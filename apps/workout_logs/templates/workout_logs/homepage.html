Your Triathlon Training

Track your progress and monitor your workouts across three key disciplines: running, swimming, and cycling. Our platform helps you log your training sessions and analyze your performance over time. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced triathlete, you'll find tools to help you improve.

Running

Log your running distances, times, and personal bests. Stay on top of your speed and endurance gains!

Tip: Focus on building a solid base before increasing your distance. Consider adding interval training to improve speed.

Benefits: Running builds cardiovascular fitness and leg strength, helping to improve your overall endurance.

Track Progress: Measure your pace, distance, and time to assess improvements. Track your runs over time and see how you progress!

Swimming

Track your swim times, lap counts, and swim intervals. Improve your form and technique in the water!

Tip: Focus on breathing techniques and stroke efficiency. Use drills to improve form and increase endurance.

Benefits: Swimming is a full-body workout that builds both strength and flexibility, with less impact on joints compared to running.

Track Progress: Measure the number of laps, total time, and stroke count to gauge improvements and set new goals.

Cycling

Log your cycling routes, times, and distances. Build strength and endurance on the bike!

Tip: Mix up your cycling sessions with interval training, hill sprints, and long endurance rides to improve both speed and stamina.

Benefits: Cycling enhances leg strength, improves cardiovascular health, and helps build muscular endurance, making it a great low-impact exercise.

Track Progress: Monitor your speed, distance, and elevation gain to understand your endurance and track performance over time.
